Course Information

CMPT 109 is dubbed Fluency in Technology because it is not just a basic Microsoft Office skills course. It is designed to be a thorough overview of the skills, concepts, and capabilities necessary to effectively use information technology in the modern world and across the curriculum.

The course has both a lab (skills) and a lecture (concepts) component.
