Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I get a permission to register into the course after it closed?
A: No. It's physically impossible. There are only 26 computers in the lab so the course cannot have more than 26 students.

Q: Do I need to buy the book?
A: If you want to use the SAM portal and the electronic content, then yes. Otherwise, no.

Q: I bought the book used. Can I still use it?
A: Yes, you just won't be able to access the optional online content.

Q: Will you assign homework directly from the book?
A: No.

Q: Will the exams include content only available in the book?
A: No.

Q: I don't have the book. What can I use to study for the exams?
A: Look on Canvas for Lecture Notes PDF document which is an extended study guide and reference material.

Q: Are the lecture slides available online?
A: Yes, they are on Canvas.

Q: Can I see sample questions from your exams?
A: Yes, look for Midterm and Final review sheets on Canvas.

Q: Do you have office hours?
A: Yes. Usually right before our scheduled class.

Q: Your office hours conflict with my schedule.
A: Contact me and schedule an appointment. I'm available on weekdays after 6pm.

Q: What's the best way to contact you?
A: Text me if it's urgent. Otherwise email.
